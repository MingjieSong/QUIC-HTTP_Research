Contacting a Representative by Phone

You can always reach an Office of Global Programs representative by phone from Monday to Friday, 9:00am to 5:00pm EST. Please call 212.998.4433.

Contacting a Representative by Zoom or Email

Use the button below to access our inquiry form.

Connect Via Zoom or Email

Use the Global Programs Contact Us Form to be contacted via email or request one-on-one Zoom appointments.

NYU Office of Global Programs answers general questions about admissions, operations, academics and preparing to enroll at the NYU academic centers and also for study away at NYU campuses in Abu Dhabi, New York, and Shanghai. Site representatives are available in New York to answer questions about all academic centers abroad. Other NYU departments answer questions about billing, student health matters, financial assistance, and immigration requirements.

To report a student emergency at any NYU location in the world, call NYU's Department of Public Safety at (US 001) 212-998-2222, (24 hours).
