Prospective Student Grad Tours

In-person tours are available for guests that are fully vaccinated and have received their booster shot. Register to learn about the schools, colleges, centers, and institutes that make up professional and graduate programs at NYU.

Learn more about our safety guidelines.

Virtual Events:

We offer a variety of informative virtual events throughout the year! Register for the sessions where we share important info about graduate and professional admissions at NYU.

Other Ways to Experience NYU

As a prospective NYU graduate or professional student, you have the opportunity to virtually tour both our Washington Square Park and Brooklyn campuses. These online tours offer a general introduction to NYUs New York City locations and facilities but do not provide school-specific academic or admissions information. If youd like to visit with a specific graduate or professional school, you should contact them directly, as opportunities across schools vary. You can also learn more about graduate and professional programs during our weekly info sessions or through a self-guided tour.

Virtual Campus Tours

Register for a Google Earth tour of NYU's Washington Square or Brooklyn campuses, narrated by a current NYU graduate student.

Webinars and Panels

Get important info about graduate and professional admissions at NYU. Featuring admissions experts and current students.

Self Guided Tour Maps and Mobile App

Complete a short form to receive access to walking maps of NYU's Washington Square and Brooklyn campuses.
Group Tours

If you are a group of 10 or more people interested in visiting NYU's Washington Square Campus, please submit our NYU Graduate Campus Tour: Group Request Form at least three weeks in advance of the date you'd like to visit. While we work as hard as possible to welcome all groups, we cannot guarantee that well be able to accommodate the date and time you request. For additional information, please email gradadmissions@nyu.eduor call212-992-GRAD (4723).

Accommodations

NYUs Office of Graduate Marketing and Admission Consulting provides reasonable accommodations to people with disabilities. Accommodation requests for events and services should be submitted at least two weeks before the date of the accommodation need. Please emailgradadmissions@nyu.eduor call212-992-GRAD (4723)for assistance.
